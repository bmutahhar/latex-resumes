You will collaborate closely with our UI/UX Design, Backend, and Product teams to ensure that users can easily interact with and configure complex risk operations workflows without needing to write code.

Design, develop, and maintain highly interactive and polished front-end components using modern web technologies.
Collaborate closely with the UI/UX design team to translate wireframes and mockups into functional, visually appealing user interfaces.
Ensure the platform’s responsiveness and cross-browser compatibility across a wide range of devices.
Identify and troubleshoot front-end performance issues, proactively implementing long-term solutions to improve user experience.
Take ownership of the front-end codebase, maintaining its quality, scalability, and reliability.
Implement best practices for front-end testing, code quality, and CI/CD pipelines.
Participate in the full product development lifecycle, from concept through delivery and post-launch improvements.
Collaborate effectively with cross-functional teams, including Product, Backend, and Infrastructure teams.
